Group rules

After finishing all group stage matches, we qualify the top team of each group - the one with highest points - to the semi-finals.

If you win a match, you get one point. Your score is how many rounds you lose subtracted from how many rounds you win (score = total rounds won - total rounds lost). Score is used ONLY if there are ties. For example, if team A and team B are equal in points after finishing all group matches, we qualify the team with more score. However, if scores are equal, they play a qualifying match.

Not following the deadlines will get you an instant loss. Play your match as soon as possible. See match discussion.
